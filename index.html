Projects

Bus Loader Project · ; February-April 2016
This SoldWorks assembly is from a group project in which we made machines that loaded small wooden people into 3D printed busses (loaded busses also pictured here). Our group consisted of four members and our machine achieved a record of 15 consecutive busses loaded without an error. My most important contributions to our group were the head-hanger concept (and 3D printed parts required) as well as the PLC programming which controled the pistons and timing. We were the only group to use a hanger-style concept and it proved very successful. The assembly below contains two sheet metal parts, six 3D printed parts, four provided neumatic cylinders andnuts/screws. I am hoping to add a video of the machine operating soon!
